About Me

I have made a profession selling custom Rubik's cubes and having my own store. I have sucessfullsy made over $1.8 million in profits. The Rubik's cube started out as a pass time, but over the years, I made my own mechanism and developed a new method of solving that earned me a world record. After a while I started to mass produce my puzzles for the cubing community. I started to slow down and have other companies sell them from their websites. I did not want to stop, so I made a wbsite to customize a Rubik's cube.


Here are some of my early designs


After my early designs, I started to focus on speedcubing rather than puzzle designing. I started to work on created my own method for solving the Rubik's cube. After a few months of hard work, I finally perfected my method. I kept practicing until I was setting unofficial world records. So I went to a competiition and broke the world record for a 3x3x3 Rubik's cube.


After the world record, I decided to give speed cubing a break and go back to my original ides of creating puzzles. I started creating more complex puzzles and started to custom make my own pieces via a 3D printer.

Once I had pefected using the 3D printer, I sold my puzzles to larger cubing stores to gain attention.

After my puzzles had sold for a while, I decided to open up my own cube shop to specialize in custom cubes for speedcubing as well as puzzle collecting.

This got boring quickly, so I decided to add a page to my site where you could custom create a Rbuik's cube to your liking. This became an isntant success which boosted my website to one of the top cube stores.
